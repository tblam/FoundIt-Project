About Us

Are you interested in buying houses?
Are you tired of looking for different websites to compare about locations, schools, crime records, flood zones, and attractions?
FoundIt site will provide all these above information in one page that aids informed housing decisions.

FoundIt designs and creates a user-friendly website that gives users accurate housing information to make their house hunting experience as smooth as possible, and would also implement features that are less inherent to buyers, but are important factors in making final decisions. The website would able to list the sources where it obtained the information, the attractions around a particular house, and a verified forum for each property where other shoppers can give their own comments. The outcome of FoundIt would enhance the research process so that buyers make educated choices when purchasing a home
